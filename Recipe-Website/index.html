Grilled chicken is a popular dish known for its flavorful, charred exterior and tender meat. It's versatile, healthy, and loved worldwide for its simple yet delicious preparation.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with the marinated chicken, tomatoes, butter, salt and spices, popular worldwide for its savory flavor & taste.

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful South Asian dish made with spiced rice, marinated meat or vegetables, and aromatic herbs. It's renowned for its rich, complex taste and fragrant aroma.

Samosas

Samosas are popular Indian snacks consisting of crispy, deep-fried pastry pockets filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat. They are enjoyed as appetizers or street food worldwide.

Panner Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes grilled or baked with spices, creating a smoky, flavorful, and vegetarian delight.
